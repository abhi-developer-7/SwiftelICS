About Us

A leading electronic payments processor with a passion for innovation: from our humble beginnings in 1995, our driving force has always been to develop turnkey payment processing solutions that take their respective markets by storm. Our curiosity and fervor for uniqueness has given us the opportunity to explore unchartered territory, with interesting results.

The Swiftel team shares one vision: to constantly deliver cutting edge solutions whatever we decide to do. This desire to explore has meant technology company, with expertise spanning everything from whitelabel retail services for some of the region’s biggest telecom operators, to PetroEasy prepaid petroleum charge cards.
Services include the following e-payment networks, e-stamps and revenue stamps and their respective vending machines and kiosks, wholesale network management and distribution as well as international calling cards. After making inroads in the services market in Kuwait, we quickly began exploring other business venues that culminated in self-service kiosks. Swiftel today is the one of its kind in its ability and expertise to manufacture and produce interactive kiosks locally, while competitors must continue to rely on imported machinery and outside assistance.
Additionally, in the process of bolstering our own business and services, we’ve managed to create a formidable distribution channel that has become the namesake of Swiftel’s success.

Today, Swiftel represents the biggest distribution partner for a variety of industries looking to make vital wholesale movements encompassing E-vouchers, E-topup, bill payment, and other future developments.
